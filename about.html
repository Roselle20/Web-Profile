EDUCATION

My academic journey began at Dalaguete Elementary School, where I developed a strong foundation for learning and earned honors. This passion for knowledge led me to Dalaguete National High School, where I graduated with high honors, a testament to my dedication and commitment to academic excellence.

PASSION

I've always been drawn to the magic of storytelling. From a young age, I found myself lost in the worlds created by my favorite authors, captivated by tales of adventure, love, and the unknown. This passion for reading eventually blossomed into a desire to create my own stories, to weave words into worlds that could transport readers to new realms and inspire their imaginations.

My novels, primarily focused on fantasy and romance, are my way of sharing those worlds with others. I love exploring the complexities of human relationships, the power of magic and myth, and the enduring themes of hope and resilience. I'm grateful that my work has found a home on Wattpad, connecting with readers who share my love for these genres.

More than just entertainment, I believe that stories have the power to teach us, to challenge our perspectives, and to leave a lasting impact on our hearts and minds. My goal as a writer is to create stories that not only captivate but also inspire, leaving readers with something to ponder long after they've turned the final page.
